About IT STAR Technology

About Us

IT STAR Technology established in 2009, IT STAR Technology is a computer repair service that specializes in the repair, maintenance, and optimization of desktops, laptops, smart devices, and servers for your home or business. Our certified technicians each have more than 10 years of experience in all fields of computing. We proudly service all Windows-based PCs and Apple Computers of every make and model.

IT STAR Technology offers a wide variety of services from PC Set-Ups and Tune-Ups and installing new Hardware and Software, to creating and repairing Wireless and Wired Networks, and removing Viruses and Spyware. We even offer services as simple as Printer Help, Email Setup and assisting you with Facebook and Twitter. IT STAR Technology will not only solve and answer any questions about your PC but also help with any electronics in your home (CCTV And Surveillance System, iDevices from Apple, Game Consoles and Home Theatres). Basic and advanced training sessions are also available to sharpen your computer skills or take you deep into the world of computing.

We offer free over-the-phone consultations and estimates and can repair your PC onsite or in our state of the art workshop. All of our work is guaranteed or your money back – there is no problem we can’t fix!

Our mission is to provide reliable service and treat our customers with courtesy and respect. Our clients are not just customers, but life long friends of our business. We strive on providing outstanding customer service and having our customers feel completely satisfied. There is no such thing about a negative experience at IT STAR Technology - if you are unhappy or the repair did not turn out as you expected, we will stop at nothing to make it right!
